Key Features

User Roles & Registration

Food Providers (colleges, hostels, hotels), Food Receivers (NGOs, orphanages, shelters), and Admins with verification system.

Food Donation Posting

Easy form to upload food details including type, quantity, expiry time, location, and photos with hygiene verification.

Smart Matching System

Auto-match nearby receivers with instant notifications, search filters, and priority tagging for urgent posts.

Pickup & Logistics

Track locations, coordinate via in-app chat/calls, and manage delivery options with real-time updates.

Hygiene & Safety

Mandatory hygiene checkboxes, quality ratings, and safety verification to ensure food safety standards.

Impact Dashboard

Track meals saved, carbon footprint reduction, leaderboards, and comprehensive analytics for all stakeholders.

About ShareBite

Our Mission

ShareBite was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create a world where no food goes to waste and no one goes hungry. We believe that every meal matters and that technology can be a force for good in connecting communities to solve one of humanity's greatest challenges.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, ShareBite emerged from witnessing the heartbreaking reality of food waste in our communities while knowing that millions of people struggle with hunger daily. We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap through innovative technology and community engagement.

What We Do

ShareBite is a comprehensive platform that connects food providers (colleges, hostels, hotels, restaurants) with food receivers (NGOs, orphanages, shelters, animal farms) to ensure surplus food reaches those who need it most. Our smart matching system, real-time notifications, and verification processes make food sharing safe, efficient, and impactful.
